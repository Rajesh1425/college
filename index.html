About Us

Gayatri +3 Science College, located in the vibrant city of Odisha, stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of science education. With a rich history and a commitment to academic advancement, Gayatri College has consistently nurtured bright minds and propelled them towards successful careers in the field of science.

Our campus is equipped with modern laboratories, libraries, and research facilities, providing students with the tools they need to explore and excel in their chosen scientific disciplines.
